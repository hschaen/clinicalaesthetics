Villa Park skincare. Our specialty.

We specialize in corrective and enhancing skincare. We have specially trained aestheticians who deal with conditions such as acne and rosacea, as well as aging skin. Our doctor and nurse can provide enhancing treatments including the judicious use of Botox, and dermal fillers and PRP. We have experienced hair removal technicians and also offer packages that include massage. Our product line includes only professional medical lines such as SkinCeuticals.

Your satisfaction. Our Philosophy.

At Clinical Aesthetics, our philosophy is that beautiful skin radiates through by harvesting the beauty from within. Clinical Aesthetics believes the best results come from a combination of treatments using both nature and science. Our extensively trained, medically supervised staff perform your personalized treatments in a spa- like setting, with the utmost concern for your comfort and satisfaction. All of our skin rejuvenation treatments are FDA approved and have been proven to reduce the visible signs of aging while improving overall skin vitality. All medical procedures are performed by licensed medical professionals under the supervision of owner, Dr. Kathleen Gallaher.
